Teaching

Wanyu's philosophy

    Wanyu loves working with students. She is lucky to have experienced firsthand the impact and significance that a mentor’s guidance and enthusiasm can have during her Ph.D. Along with research, this mentor-student relationship is one of the main reasons she is pursuing a career in academia.

    Being a runner for so many years, Wanyu knows the value of persistence and effort. She always encourages students to solve problems with an engineering mindset, which is not limited to solving technical challenges but can also be applied to all aspects of life. She believes that helping students build skills and confidence are equally important, especially for first-year engineering students.

    As a teacher, her goal is to help students build the skills and the confidence they need to tackle the unknown. The world of computing is changing rapidly, and students need to be adaptable fast-learners to succeed in their careers.

    Courses Wanyu has been teaching in the 2021 - 2023 academic year at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University:
  • COMP6434 Big Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence
  • COMP5511 Artificial Intelligence Concepts
  • COMP1012 Programming Fundamentals and Applications
